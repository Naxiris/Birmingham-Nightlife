    • What is the nightscene?


    The Nightscene is a webpage developed to show others some fun places to visit in Birmingham. Bars are fun and a great place to socialize so here are some links to get you started on your adventures! But be safe and responsible in these places, they're meant to be fun for everybody.

    Tips and tricks for a night out!

    When leaving for a night out, perhaps take some precautions and read over a code of conduct of sorts to ensure you get the most enjoyment out of your night.

    Here are some examples of things to watch out for;

    • Always care for your drink.
    • Treat others with respect.
    • Steer clear of aggressive people.
    • If you're unable to walk, take a taxi.
